Usage

How does it Work?

Manager Interface

A powerful dashboard for gym owners to manage memberships, track attendance, and automate renewals effortlessly.

How it works

  • Sign In & Setup: Register your gym and customize business settings.

  • Create & Manage Memberships: Offer session-based, day-based, and standard plans.

  • Monitor Attendance & Renewals: Track check-ins, automate renewals, and send reminders.

  • Analyse Business Performance: Get insights on revenue, member engagement, and trends.

Member Interface

A user-friendly app that makes gym check-ins, session tracking, and membership renewals easy for members.

How it works

  • Login & View Membership: Customers access their plans and usage history.

  • Check-In with QR Code/PIN: Fast and secure session redemption.

  • Track Sessions & Notifications: Stay updated on attendance and renewal alerts.

  • Renew & Pay Anytime: Seamless online/offline payments for continued access.

Business Webpage

An interactive, SEO-optimized webpage where potential customers can explore, enroll, and make secure payments effortlessly.

How it works

  • Explore Gym Offerings: Customers browse services, trainers, and membership plans.

  • Choose & Enrol Online: Select the preferred plan and complete enrolment via the website.

  • Instant App Access: After payment, customers receive a link to download the app.

  • Secure Payments & Direct Transfers: All payments are processed securely and transferred directly to the gym’s bank account.
